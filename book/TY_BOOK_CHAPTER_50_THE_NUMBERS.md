|---|---|---|

| **Most likely** | **approx. 1,060 hours** | **132 full 8-hour work days** |

Across 319 days, the most likely estimate is **1,060 active hours** — an average of **3.3 hours every single day**, including weekends, holidays, and every other day in between.

### What These Numbers Do Not Include

The timestamp method captures only time inside the chat interface. It does not count:

- Time running PowerShell commands between messages
- Time in Lovable building and testing SilverSounds321
- Time reviewing Lovable's output before pasting it back
- Time running cargo check, cargo test, and git operations
- Time testing the live site at silversounds321.com in a browser
- Time reading handoff documents at the start of each session
- Time thinking between sessions

The real hours invested in TY AI OS and SilverSounds321 across 319 days are conservatively **1,200–1,500 hours** or more when all working time is included. The timestamp calculation captures what can be proven. The full reality is larger.
