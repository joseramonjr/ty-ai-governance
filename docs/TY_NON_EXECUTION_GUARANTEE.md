## I. What This Document Is

This is a formal builder declaration. It states, in the builder's own words
and with full personal authority, that TYOVA -- the TY AI OS Integrity Hub --
does not execute TY AI OS under any condition at this time.

This document cannot be rewritten by any AI system. It cannot be amended by
any automated process. It is a human statement, recorded permanently in the
governance repository, about what TYOVA is and what it is not.

---

## II. What TYOVA Is

TYOVA was made for everyone to read and understand the TY ecosystem.

I wanted everyone to know and understand how TY was developed -- to help
humanity understand more about the AI world of thinking. TYOVA is a
documentation history of the TY AI OS ecosystem. It is also the only place
I have officially designated for people to find the authentic version of TY AI
OS -- not an unauthorized version.

TYOVA was made to be the first place to have all documented history of TY AI
OS in a centralized location. Anyone in the world can visit it, read it, and
understand what TY is, how it was built, and what it stands for -- without
needing access to any repository, any runtime, or any executable component.

---

## III. What TYOVA Is Not

As of the date of this document, TYOVA is not running TY AI OS.

TYOVA does not execute. It does not control anything. It does not govern
anything. It does not issue commands to any component of the TY ecosystem.

TYOVA is a read-only canonical archive. Every page you read on TYOVA is
documentation -- not execution. Every chapter in the Book of TY is a record
of what was built -- not a running system. Every governance document published
through TYOVA is a reference -- not an active enforcement mechanism.

This is not a limitation. It is a design decision made deliberately by the
builder.

---

## IV. Why This Matters

I made TYOVA inspection-only because I wanted there to be a place where
anyone could read and understand TY AI OS without any risk that the act of
reading would trigger execution.

Trust must be earned before authority is granted. TYOVA earns trust by being
transparent -- by showing everything about how TY was built, what its
invariants are, and what it stands for. That transparency only works if TYOVA
itself is inert. A documentation system that executes code is not a
documentation system -- it is a runtime in disguise.

TYOVA is not a runtime in disguise. It is what it says it is: a canonical
archive. Nothing more. Nothing less.

---

## V. The Future

I will eventually include TY AI OS into TYOVA in a more integrated way in the
near future. When that happens, it will be done deliberately, with full
governance documentation, and with the same discipline that has governed every
other step of TY AI OS development.

That future integration will not happen silently. It will not happen
automatically. It will be a builder decision, recorded in the governance
repository, with a CLO, a commit, and a ledger entry.

Until that decision is made and recorded, this guarantee stands: TYOVA does
not execute TY AI OS.

**TYOVA is inspection-only. It presents. It does not execute. It does not
