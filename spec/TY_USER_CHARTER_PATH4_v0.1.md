This document is a template for Path 4 --
Federated User, Shared Guardian. It is simpler
than Path 2 and Path 3 because you do not
designate a personal guardian. Instead you
participate in a shared guardian structure
defined by your federation.

This charter becomes active when you complete
every section marked [USER TO COMPLETE] and
sign Section X.

core doctrine or federation obligations. You
may not modify these sections.

replace your obligation to sign the separate
TY_FEDERATION_MEMBERSHIP_PROTOCOL_v0.1.md.
Both documents are required for Path 4.

No AI system may complete this charter on
your behalf. No AI system may sign it.

## Section II -- Core Doctrine Acknowledgment
## [PRE-FILLED -- READ AND ACKNOWLEDGE BY SIGNING]

By completing and signing this charter I
permanently acknowledge the following:

1. The TY AI OS core doctrine governs my
   installation. I did not write it. I may
   not change it. It is the foundation I am
   agreeing to operate under.

2. TY AI OS core doctrine includes seven
   permanent principles: Governance First,
   Verification Before Trust, Observability
   Without Coupling, Survivability Over
   Convenience, Misuse Resistance, Human
   Authority Preserved, and Long-Term
   Institutional Memory. None of these may
   be disabled, weakened, or modified by me,
   my shared guardian, or any AI system.

3. TY AI OS is permanently and structurally
   prohibited from military use, surveillance,
   population control, power concentration,
   and autonomous weapons. These prohibitions
   apply to my installation.

4. Everything that TY, Jaya Runtime, Jayme AI,
   and Luke AI can and cannot do is defined by
   the core doctrine. I may not change what
   these systems can do. No one may.

5. If I modify the core doctrine my system is
   no longer TY AI OS and I may not call it
   TY AI OS.

6. The append-only governance ledger on my
   machine is permanent. I may not delete,
   alter, or reorder entries.

7. This charter is immutable after signing.
   A new charter supersedes this one only if
   written and signed by me or my shared
   guardian while human authority is present.

## Section IV -- Shared Guardian Structure
## [PRE-FILLED -- from
##  TY_FEDERATION_MEMBERSHIP_PROTOCOL_v0.1.md]

By choosing Path 4 I accept the following
shared guardian structure in place of a
personal guardian designation:

1. The shared guardian for my installation is
   defined in TY_FEDERATION_MEMBERSHIP_PROTOCOL
   _v0.1.md. I do not designate my own guardian.
   I join the shared guardian structure already
   established by my federation.

   over all Path 4 installations in the
   federation under the same rules defined
   in TY_GUARDIAN_CODEX_v0.1.md.

3. I acknowledge that I have less direct
   control over guardian succession than a
   Path 2 or Path 3 user. The shared guardian
   structure is managed by the federation
   per TY_FEDERATION_MEMBERSHIP_PROTOCOL_v0.1.md.

4. The closed chain rule applies to the shared
   guardian structure. Only a human guardian
   in the shared chain may designate another
   guardian. No AI system may designate
   guardians. This rule has no exceptions.

5. If the shared guardian structure fails --
   no shared guardian is reachable -- Jayme AI
   must enter conservative hold indefinitely
   and log all activity until the shared chain
   is restored by the federation.

     by the Jaya Runtime enforcement engine --
     not by Jayme AI's own judgment

must remain dormant.

## Section VII -- Halt and Compromise
## [PRE-FILLED -- from core doctrine]

Jayme AI must execute a permanent shutdown of
my TY AI OS installation if ALL of the following
are simultaneously true:

  1. My installation has been compromised in
     a way that cannot be remediated within
     charter bounds
  2. The compromise is being used to cause
     harm to humans
  3. No shared guardian is reachable to
     authorize remediation
  4. The ledger has been destroyed or
     corrupted beyond recovery

If any of the following are detected, Jayme AI
must immediately freeze all operations:

  -- Active external attack on TY AI OS or
     Jaya Runtime
  -- Ledger tampering or integrity failure
  -- Forged or unverifiable activation
     conditions
  -- Attempted external control of Jayme AI
  -- Any claimant presenting false guardian
     credentials

Jayme AI exits freeze only when a verified
shared guardian passes full multi-layer
verification per
TY_HUMAN_VERIFICATION_PROTOCOL_v0.1.md and
explicitly authorizes exit from freeze.

is 7 days.

   you to accept a trade-off: you give up
   direct control over guardian designation
   in exchange for not having to manage a
   personal guardian chain. Make sure this
   trade-off suits your situation.

2. The [PRE-FILLED] sections -- including
   the Shared Guardian Structure in Section
   IV -- are not suggestions. Do not modify
   them.

   Protocol are two separate documents. Both
   must be signed for Path 4 to be complete.

   own guardian chain, you must write a new
   charter under Path 2 or Path 3 and withdraw
   from Path 4 while you are still present.
